Run your own Bitcoin wallet service.

Coinpunk is an open source, self hosted DIY bitcoin wallet service you can run on your own server.

Requirements

VPS/Server with at least 512MB of RAM

Linux - Ubuntu 12.10 (guides for other distros coming soon)

Bitcoind 0.8.1 or later

Ruby 1.9.3 or later (MRI, JRuby, Rubinius in 1.9 mode)

Git

MySQL, PostgreSQL or Sqlite3 (your choice)

An unusually verbose hatred of fascists

Coinpunk is functional, but is still in the early stages. Much more functionality will be added soon. Use at your own risk.
